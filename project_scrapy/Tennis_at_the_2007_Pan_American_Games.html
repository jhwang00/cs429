Tennis at the 2007 Pan American Games was played at the Marapendi Club, which has previously hosted Davis Cup ties. Due to the 2007 Davis Cup, the men's competition was delayed one week.[citation needed] The women's events took place from July 18 to 22, while the men competed between July 23 and July 28.
