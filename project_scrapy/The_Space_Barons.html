The Space Barons: Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, and the Quest to Colonize the Cosmos
AuthorChristian Davenport
CountryUnited States
LanguageEnglish
GenreNon-fiction
PublisherPublicAffairs
Publication date
March 20, 2018
Pages336
ISBN978-1-61039-829-9

The Space Barons: Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, and the Quest to Colonize the Cosmos is a 2018 book by Christian Davenport. It covers the rise of the commercial space companies Blue Origin, led by Amazon founder Jeff Bezos, and SpaceX, led by PayPal and Tesla Motors founder Elon Musk.[1][2][3]
